friends help friends

Sometimes we forget that everything we do sets off a chain reaction because we seem so small on this planet. Life is easy with shelves stocked high at supermarkets, but do you realize how detrimental plastic is to the environment? It's easy to grab what's there, but many species of fish are close to extinction because of our ease of access to their friends.
